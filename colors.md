# Colors

Colors are generated with [Atmos](https://app.atmos.style/6722908be2129fc5e2110e65).

Color shades follow these lightness rules:
- whites: ease-in-out from 8.5 to 91.5
- neutrals: ease-in-out from 10 to 90
- orange and lime: linear from 18 to 90
- rose, cyan and indigo: linear from 10 to 90
